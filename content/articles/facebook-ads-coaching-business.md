## Why Most Coaching Facebook Ads Fail

Before we look at what works, we must diagnose what doesn't. When coaches run their own ads, they typically make three fatal errors:

### 1. The Direct-to-Call Pitch
Running an ad that says, "I am a certified [life coach](/coaches/life-coaching), book a free [discovery call](/learn/coaching-discovery-call-script) with me today!" is the fastest way to lose money. Cold traffic does not want to get on the phone with a stranger. You have not earned the right to ask for 45 minutes of their time. 

### 2. The Generic Lead Magnet
Offering a "10 Tips for a Better Life" PDF will get you cheap leads, but those leads will never buy. Generic lead magnets attract freebie seekers, not qualified buyers. Your lead magnet must solve a highly specific, painful micro-problem.

### 3. Ignoring the Backend Nurture
If a prospect downloads your free guide, what happens next? If the answer is "nothing," your funnel is broken. High-ticket coaching requires a significant amount of trust. That trust is built through a sophisticated email nurture sequence that follows the initial opt-in.

## The Anatomy of a Profitable Facebook Coaching Funnel

To succeed with coaching Facebook ads, you need a multi-step funnel that moves a prospect from unaware to highly interested. The most proven architecture is the **Lead Magnet to VSL Funnel**.

### Step 1: The Ad
The ad is designed to do one thing: get a qualified prospect to stop scrolling and click. It should offer a high-value, low-friction lead magnet (e.g., a cheat sheet, a diagnostic quiz, or a template).

### Step 2: The Opt-In Page
A simple, distraction-free landing page where they exchange their email address for the lead magnet. 

### Step 3: The Video Sales Letter (VSL)
Once they opt in, they are immediately redirected to a page featuring a 10-15 minute video. This video delivers the value promised in the ad, but it also shifts their paradigm, exposes a larger problem they didn't realize they had, and pitches a discovery call as the solution to that larger problem.

### Step 4: The Application Page
Before they can book a call, they must fill out an application. This filters out the tire kickers and ensures you only speak with qualified prospects who have the financial means to hire you.

### Step 5: The Email Nurture
For the 80% of people who don't book a call immediately, an automated 7-day [email sequence](/learn/coaching-email-nurture-sequence) delivers case studies, handles objections, and continuously pushes them back to the VSL and booking page.

## Mastering Facebook Ad Targeting for Coaches

In the past, coaches would spend hours narrowing down their targeting to hyper-specific interests. Post-iOS 14, the game has changed. The algorithm is now smarter than your manual targeting.

### The Shift to Broad Targeting
Instead of trying to find the exact intersection of 5 different obscure interests, use broad targeting. Let the Facebook machine learning algorithm do the heavy lifting. 

How does the algorithm know who to show the ad to if the targeting is broad? **Your copy and your creative do the targeting.**

If your ad starts with, "Attention 6-Figure Agency Owners struggling with client churn...", the algorithm will quickly learn that only agency owners interact with the ad, and it will optimize delivery automatically.

### Lookalike Audiences
If you have an existing email list of clients or a pixel tracking visitors to your website, Lookalike Audiences are your best friend. Facebook will analyze the data points of your current clients and find millions of other users with similar behavioral profiles. A 1% Lookalike of your buyer list is often the highest-converting audience you can target.

## Creating Ads That Stop the Scroll

Your ad creative is the most critical element of your front-end strategy. It must be disruptive.

### The Hook is Everything
You have exactly 3 seconds to capture attention. 
*   **Bad Hook:** "Hi, I'm John and I'm a [business coach](/coaches/business-coaching)."
*   **Good Hook:** "If you are working 60 hours a week and your revenue hasn't grown in 6 months, your operational structure is broken."

### Long-Form Copy vs. Short-Form
For high-ticket coaching, long-form copy almost always outperforms short-form. Why? Because reading a 500-word ad requires commitment. If a prospect reads the entire ad, they are highly qualified by the time they click. Use storytelling, agitate their pain points, and provide immediate value in the text itself.

### Video vs. Image
You should test both, but video generally converts better for coaches because it allows the prospect to experience your energy and communication style. Keep the video raw and authentic. Highly produced, cinematic videos often look like traditional commercials, triggering "ad blindness." A video shot on your iPhone while walking often performs significantly better.

## Tracking and Optimization

You cannot scale what you do not measure. To run a profitable Facebook coaching funnel, you must track your metrics obsessively. 

*   **Cost Per Click (CPC):** Tells you if your ad creative is engaging.
*   **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** Tells you if your landing page is converting.
*   **Cost Per Call Booked:** Tells you if your VSL and email nurture are effective.
*   **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Tells you if your entire system is profitable.

If your CPA is $500, but your coaching package is $5,000, you have a money-printing machine. You can spend $500 all day long to acquire $5,000 clients.
